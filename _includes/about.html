What is Wichitalks?

Wichitalks brings together the passionate individuals, high-performers, and creative minds in our community to present on what drives them. It provides a glimpse into the greater Wichita identity. At each event, 10 individuals present in a rapid-fire environment, one after the next. With only 20 slides, each auto-advancing every 15 seconds, presentations are brief, fun, insightful, and passionately delivered. Presenters are individuals in Wichita who have a passion to share. They may be business owners, volunteers, or life-hackers. The goal is to teach something of social value and spread ideas.

So, if you'd like to learn more about the people in your city, Wichitalks is a great place to start.

We hope to see you there.
